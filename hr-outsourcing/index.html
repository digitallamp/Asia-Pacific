HR Outsourcing

Effective recruitment andHRneeds to be led by an expert. It takes time and money to hone and develop the correct set of skills necessary, and employing and maintaining a department responsible for managinghuman resourcescan be a strain on your financial resources; especially for smaller businesses.

Whether you need home-based remote HR Staff or an office-based HR support team, look no further than Outsourced. Your dedicated offshore staff can either work from home or from our state-of-the-art office in Hisar, India

Human resources outsourcing services offer a practical, cost-effective, high-quality alternative to hiring a full department or utilizing consultants. Asia Pacific HR Consultants provides human resource solutions to businesses through our team of HR specialists. Our HR consultants are available to handle anything you need from advice to managing your recruitment process on your behalf and even acting as an in-house HR department would do; by writing policies and managing the employees and culture within your organization.

We have experience hiring various remote HR roles such as:

  • Human Resources Manager
  • HR Director
  • Human Resources Assistant
  • Human Resources Officer
  • HR generalist
  • HR/Administration Officer
  • HR Advisor

Asia Pacific HR Consultants understands that every business has unique needs and requirements, so you get a staff member dedicated to you and your teams needs.
